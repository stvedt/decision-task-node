Decision-making Game

Instructions

Welcome to this decision-making game. In this task, you will be presented with 8 different choice problems. Each problem will consist of two different options which you must choose from. You can start each problem by sampling repeatedly between both options without earning or losing any money. Once you are ready to make a decision, please press on either one of the two options followed by the "final" button at the center of the page.

Please note, the task will be over once you complete all 8 problems. If you are happy to proceed, then click on the "play" button below.
